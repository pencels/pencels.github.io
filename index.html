Hello there!

My name is Christopher Pence. I go to school at UC San Diego, with a major in Computer Science, and a minor in Mathematics. Among my interests in CS are linguistics, drawing and graphic design.

As of winter 2017, I work for the CSE Department at UCSD, tutoring for CSE 30 (Computer Organization and Systems Programming). I started with ARM in the fall, and am doing SPARC now.
I am fascinated by low-level programming and all its intricacies, and can't wait to learn more about the stuff at the hardware-level this quarter!

Last summer (2016), I was working at the WhizKidz Computer Center as an instructor in mainly web development and game development.

I'll hopefully have this website better fleshed out sooner or later. Thank you, dear websurfer, for taking interest in my small place on the internet.
